EDA of Europe's air traffic data, gotten from >
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/samithsachidanandan/air-traffic-in-europe-from-2016-to-2024/data

All CSV files contain data about the time of the flights (year, month name and number, date; columns), place (airport and country),
and what type of flight (number of arrivals, departures, total, IFR). 

The goal of the analysis was to see if certain relationships existed between data, what changed over the years, how do certain countries
comapre to others and to give more insight into those relationships for possible future analyses.
